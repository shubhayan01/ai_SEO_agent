Frequently Asked Questions

What is term insurance?

Pays your nominee a lump sum if you die during the policy term. No maturity payout.

How much do I need?

10-15x annual income plus outstanding debts. Most planners recommend at least Rs 1 Cr for someone earning Rs 8-10 lakh.

Is a medical test required?

Required above Rs 50 lakh coverage or age 45+. Below that, self-declaration is usually enough.
