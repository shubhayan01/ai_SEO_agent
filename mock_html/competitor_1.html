How to Choose

  1. Check the Claim Settlement Ratio
  2. Compare premiums for your age
  3. Evaluate available riders

Riders Explained

  • Critical Illness Rider - adds ~Rs 250-500/month
  • Accidental Death Benefit - doubles payout
  • Waiver of Premium on disability

Tax Benefits

Section 80C deduction up to Rs 1.5 lakh. Death benefit tax-free under Section 10(10D).

Frequently Asked Questions

Which plan has the highest claim settlement ratio?

Max Life at 99.35%, followed by LIC at 98.62%.

What coverage do I need?

At least 10-15x your annual income.

Can NRIs buy term insurance in India?

Yes, with passport, visa, and overseas address proof.

Is online better than offline?

Online plans are 20-30% cheaper with no agent commission.

What if I miss a premium?

30-day grace period. Policy lapses after that.
